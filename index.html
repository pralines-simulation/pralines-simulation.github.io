The PRALINES Project

Giant molecular clouds are sites where stars form, produce metals, and drive winds that determine the fate of galaxies.
However, we do not know much about physical processes occurring on this scale, calling for a systematic study on the evolution of GMCs.

Our team is particularly interested in feedback processes that are responsible for the regulation of star formation, such as photo-ionization and stellar winds, the escape of ionizing radiation

A suite of turbulent magnetized GMC simulations with radiation, stellar winds, and supernova performed with an adaptive mesh refinement code, RAMSES-RT

Kimm et al. (2022) accepted
